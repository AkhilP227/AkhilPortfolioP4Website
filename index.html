Healthy relationships with my friends and family are important to me and with all the pressure from school I had to manage my time very efficiently. Through my various hardships in my classes, sports, and at home, I have learned my best learning styles and my largest areas for improvement. Throughout the past fifteen years, I have grown, adapted, and learned much more about myself and who I want to be. As I continue to face new challenges and fail more times, I will continuously improve as well. Every obstacle I overcome is a sign of growth and will take me one step closer to my goal of accomplishing creative innovation in the field of technology and AI.
